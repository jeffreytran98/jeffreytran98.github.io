Hey! Thanks for stopping by my website! I'm Jeffrey, a UC Berkeley alumni (c/o 2021) where I earned a Bachelors in Cognitive Science and a Minor in Data Science! During my time at Berkeley, I played mellophone in the Cal Band and was team captain for our Division 1 CS:GO & Valorant team.

Early Career: Writing "Early Career" is funny to me because of how young I am in my career. My first full-time job was as a Search Associate, essentially a Recruiting Coordinator, for Frederickson Partners, one of the best Executive Search Firms out there. After about, I wanted to explore the tech space and found Embark Trucks, an AI Trucking company based in San Francisco, California, where I worked as a Recruiting Coordinator. Unfortunately, my time as an RC was shortlived as the Company decided to pause hiring efforts during our hyper-growth stage only a few months in. As a result, myself and a handful of others were moved to different parts of the company. I was moved to our Deep Learning Data QA team where I worked closely with Machine Learning Engineers and other QA Specialists. I was able to utilize my technical experience I had gained from undergrad for the first time by writing python scripts and performed data analysis tasks. I was learning so much everyday but everything came to an abrupt stop on March 3rd, 2023 when Embark Trucks announced a company wide layoff that affected 70% of us.

Currently: So what am I up to now? Ultimately, my time at Embark has reignited my love and passion to work with code. I took a small break after being laid off but I've recently started to hone in on my technical skills to become a potential analyst or software engineer by taking courses on UDemy, LeetCoding, and working on Personal Projects such as this website you are currently on.
